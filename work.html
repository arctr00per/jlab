CLIENT: OVERDYE

Overdye is a curated marketplace for high-end, artisanal clothing, carrying brands like Visvim and Orslow whose garments represent the highest standards of craft and, in many cases, the preservation of ancient dyeing and manufacturing traditions. The brand exists to make sure no piece goes unworn and no history is forgotten. I worked with the team on illustrations and graphics for ad campaigns designed to surface those stories and connect with customers who care as deeply about provenance as they do about the clothes themselves.
